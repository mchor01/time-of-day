Time of Day

A JavaScript project by Micah Chor

Greeting goes here

This page demonstrates how to use JavaScript to manipulate webpages based on the time of the day.

Depending on the time of day, Morning, Afternoon, Evening, or Night, the webpage will alter according to time by changing the picture and background color of the page.

The copyright displayed at the bottom of the page will also be kept up to date and will display the current year.
